Getting Started with Modern MCU

A comprehensive guide for IoT enthusiasts

Posted by JagTechno Team on April 2025

The Modern MCU is a powerful microcontroller with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth built in, making it ideal for IoT projects. In this guide, we’ll walk through setup, tools, and basic coding to get your first Modern MCU project running.

Why Choose Modern MCU?

  • Dual-core processor: High performance for complex tasks.
  • Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth: Ready for IoT connectivity.
  • Low power consumption: Essential for battery-operated devices.
  • Wide community support: Extensive libraries and tutorials.

Getting Started Steps

  1. Install Arduino IDE or PlatformIO: Choose your preferred development environment.
  2. Select Modern MCU board: Configure the IDE for your specific board (e.g., Modern MCU Dev Module).
  3. Connect and upload: Write a simple "Blink" sketch and upload it to verify the connection.

Conclusion

The Modern MCU opens up endless possibilities for IoT innovation. Stay tuned for future tutorials and use cases!
